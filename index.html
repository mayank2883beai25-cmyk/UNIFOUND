FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does verification take?
Most items are reviewed and verified within 24–48 hours by campus administrators. You'll receive an email notification as soon as the status changes.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Your information is protected using secure authentication and is only accessible to authorized campus staff.
Can I edit my report after submission?
You can update certain details after submission depending on your report status and your campus verification process.
Who verifies the items?
Verified campus administrators or designated lost-and-found staff review and confirm item details.
What if no one finds my item?
Your report stays active in the system, and you'll be notified if a matching item is submitted later.
